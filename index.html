Lake and Pool Access

Guests enjoy access to Sleepy Hollow Lake as well as community pools. Guests can use the lake for swimming and fishing. The closest lake access is a 3 minute drive or a 10 minute walk. The closest pool is a 3 minute drive or a 20 minute walk.

Community Amenities

The community has picnic areas as well as tennis, pickleball, volleyball, bocce ball, and basketball courts. There is also a driving range. You just bring the equipment and we'll provide the access codes to each of the facilities. There are also playgrounds.

Accomodates up to Ten People

The house has comfortable queen-sized beds in each of the four bedrooms. For bookings of nine or ten guests, inflatable mattresses are provided according to the number of guests booked. Bookings of eight or fewer do not include inflatable mattresses.

Nearby Attractions

Only 20 minutes to Hudson, 15 minutes to Catskill, and 2.5 hours from New York City. About 40 minutes to Windham and 45 minutes to Hunter. Kaaterskill Falls and other great hikes are only 40 minutes away.
